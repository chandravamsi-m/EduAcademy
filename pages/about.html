Our Story

Our Teaching Story & Philosophy

EduAcademy commenced in 2016 as a small project with a core vision: giving high school and university candidates access to professional, dedicated mentors who could simplify complex concepts in math, tech, and languages.

Today, we are proud to support thousands of active members globally by combining modern digital tools (webboards, tracking charts, and homework grading flows) with handpicked teaching talent.
